DU PLSR

The PLSR is a 4-pattern, 16-step stochastic drum sequencer. In addition to being enabled and disabled as in a traditional sequencer, each step can also be set to marks 1 through 4, which trigger with probability determined by the corresponding CV input.

Clock Out

Primary clock output.

Clock In

Primary clock input. Internal clock is used when disconnected.

Reset In

Reset trigger input. Immediately resets the pattern (and internal clock, if using).

Probability 1 In

Controls the probability of steps marked 1 triggering (0V to 5V range).

Probability 2 In

Controls the probability of steps marked 2 triggering (0V to 5V range).

Probability 3 In

Controls the probability of steps marked 3 triggering (0V to 5V range).

Probability 4 In

Controls the probability of steps marked 4 triggering (0V to 5V range).

Pattern 1 Out

Trigger output for sequencer pattern 1.

Pattern 2 Out

Trigger output for sequencer pattern 2.

Pattern 3 Out

Trigger output for sequencer pattern 3.

Pattern 4 Out

Trigger output for sequencer pattern 4.

Save Box

Double-click to jump to the save box. Click to save the current envelope values to memory.

Step Count

Scroll to select the number of steps to count (1 to 16) when sequencing.

Clock Rate

Scroll to modify the internal clock rate, in beats per minute (up to 300 BPM). Displays EXT if overridden by the external clock input.

Swing

Scroll to modify the amount to swing the clock beat (starting from 50% straight time), up to 74% in increments of 6%.

Pattern 1

Displays the selected steps for pattern 1. Scroll to navigate, click to modify the step (cycles through off, on, and marks 1 through 4), double-click to jump to pattern 2.

Pattern 2

Displays the selected steps for pattern 2. Scroll to navigate, click to modify the step (cycles through off, on, and marks 1 through 4), double-click to jump to pattern 3.

Pattern 3

Displays the selected steps for pattern 3. Scroll to navigate, click to modify the step (cycles through off, on, and marks 1 through 4), double-click to jump to pattern 4.

Pattern 4

Displays the selected steps for pattern 4. Scroll to navigate, click to modify the step (cycles through off, on, and marks 1 through 4), double-click to jump to the top menu.

Step Cursor

Indicates the current output step while sequencing.

Quarter Note Ruler

Subdivides the 16 step bar into 4 quarter notes as a visual reference.
